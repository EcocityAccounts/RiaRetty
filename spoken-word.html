Spoken Word

Ria Retty

 

Food is something essential

Food is something essential . The kitchen to  the cook with passion offers love to all ; family,friends,strangers........

Me honey , me love ,

Welcome to me pot ,

Tasty chicken it do have ,

Chicken sizzle hot hot ,

Me kitchen never empty ,

Me kitchen like me love ,

Will always be there ,

I know your tummy grambling ,

De reason why  you mumbling ,

In me kitchen eggs me scrambling ,

I know your worms dem gambling ,

Sit down be patient ,
